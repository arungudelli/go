[go1.16rc1](https://golangtutorial.dev/news/g-1.16rc1-released/) Release candidate of Go 1.16 was released two weeks ago.

There are currently [5 minor issues](https://github.com/golang/go/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+milestone%3AGo1.16) in the Go 1.16. 

And Golang team decided to release the final version of **Go 1.16 on coming Tuesday. i.e, 16-02-2021**.
